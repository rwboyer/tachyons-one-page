Photographic Workshops

RW Boyer

From time to time we host open photographic workshops in the mid-Atlantic USA, Spain, and the UK. Our workshops are always about hands on learning for participants. We believe the best way to aquire new skills is in doing, not watching someone else do it. We offer a variety of topics, never the same workshop twice. Workshops range from lighting to talent direction to story telling. All of them have one thing in common; They demonstrate that all of the discrete elements have to come together in order to tell a story greater than the sum of the parts.

Sign-up for our newsletter for an on-going schedule of locations and topics. Or reach out and we'll point you towards venues calendars where we will be.
